Beginner Arabic Lessons

Our beginner course is designed to introduce you to the fundamentals of Arabic language. Each lesson builds upon the previous one, creating a solid foundation for your Arabic learning journey.

Course Overview

This course is designed to introduce you to the Arabic alphabet and sound system, providing a solid foundation for your Arabic learning journey. By the end of this course, you'll be able to recognize, pronounce, and write all Arabic letters in their various forms.

What You'll Learn

  • All 28 Arabic letters and their pronunciation
  • Types of Arabic letters
  • Short and long vowels in Arabic
  • Basic reading and writing skills

Course Details

  • Duration: 4 weeks
  • 8 comprehensive lessons
  • Suitable for complete beginners
  • Certificate upon completion
